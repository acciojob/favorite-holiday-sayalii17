Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in the Himalayas, known for its scenic landscapes, adventure sports, and vibrant culture. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and explorers.

  1. Solang Valley - Adventure hub for skiing and paragliding
  2. Hidimba Devi Temple- Famous ancient cave temple set in a cedar forest
  3. Beas River- Ideal for scenic walks and river rafting
  4. Jogini Waterfalls- Stellar trek and picnic spot with cascading falls
